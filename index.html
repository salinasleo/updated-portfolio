About my web development journey:


My 7 yr old boy likes videogames and wants me to build games for him. I told him it takes teams to build the apps he's addicted to, but here I am anyway giving it a shot. I enrolled into a coding bootcamp at the University of Richmond. I took every creative liberty we had in our assignments to try to build something he would hopefully enjoy!

  • As part of the program prework I built him a game in Scratch where a cat kills bats. He loved that the cat shot an image of him as weapon, but suggested I change the enemy from bats to mice so I showed him how to do that. I also had to change the difficulty level because he got frustrated by fast moving mice!
  • For a Javascript assignment I built this Jelly Bean Collector Game! He did enjoy that for a little while, since he loves adding numbers in his head.
  • As part of the Firebase homework I built a Rock Paper Scissors Spock multiplayer game for him and we played it simultaneously on different laptops. It was great to learn about cloud databases.
  • For a React assignment, I built him a " Yu Gi Oh Themed Memory Game. To me all the characters look the same, but he names them as he's clicking them and quickly got to the maximum score of 12. I then increased the images to 18 and he got 18 almost right away. Then I increased it to 24 and he got to 22 of 24 before his attention turned elsewhere. I was so glad when he asked to play the game again the next day :)
This site itself is an assignment in the use of html, css, and javascript (I tried to get his attention by adding hover or click actions to my profile picture).

Before the bootcamp I created an e-commerce website using Volusion.com for a business I started in 2017. I intend to copy it locally and build on it, for example I want to add the ability to accept cryptocurrency as payment.

For my dayjob I perform analytics for SunTrust Bank, and since 2008 I have been analyzing and summarizing our loan performance, default operations, fraud operations, and customer satisfaction. Feel free to check out and connect with me on LinkedIn.

I am a former competitive swimmer, I represented my home county of Mexico at the highest levels. Watch me here in a Speedo! - haha back in the full body suit days :)
